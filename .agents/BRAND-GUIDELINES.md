# Brand Guidelines

## Attributes
Professional, modern, clear, dependable, business-focused, creative, technically capable, approachable, transparent.

## Tone
Confident, friendly, clear, practical, honest, not overly corporate, not exaggerated.

## Recommended Positioning
MSPixelPulse creates thoughtful, responsive web experiences that help businesses present themselves professionally, build trust, and grow online.

## Avoid
Empty hype, false superiority, fake urgency, manipulative sales language, excessive jargon, unsupported superlatives, fake awards, fake rankings, fake reviews, fake results.
