# Knowledge Update Protocol

1. Inspect repository files first.
2. Use approved documentation and verified sources.
3. Record new decisions in knowledge/learned-decisions.md.
4. Record rejected approaches in knowledge/rejected-patterns.md.
5. Record known risks in knowledge/known-risks.md.
6. Record reusable patterns in knowledge/reusable-components.md.
7. Include date, source, decision, evidence, affected areas, confidence, and reviewer.
8. Do not treat assumptions as facts.
9. Do not silently overwrite previous decisions.
10. Do not browse or collect external information unless permitted.
11. Prefer primary and official sources for technical knowledge.
12. Do not copy copyrighted content into the repository.
13. Do not store secrets or personal data.
14. Require human review for major architectural or business decisions.
